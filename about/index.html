Why d(ate)?

D(ate) is helping you explore Toronto the way you should. We created D(ate) because we’re often overwhelmed by the selection of bars, venues, restaurants, cafes and points of interests that Toronto has to offer.

We have travelled to areas across the city looking for places that we believe to be unique and enjoyable. Our aim is to produce guides that would benefit Toronto readers and modern travellers alike, recommending a tight selection of experiences locally. You’ll soon notice that our version of the perfect date is somewhat traditional with an urban flair. We will be featuring cheap eats along with fine dining, allowing for no defined budget. We are here to show you the do’s and don’ts of Toronto, through our experiences. Not intended to be comprehensive.
