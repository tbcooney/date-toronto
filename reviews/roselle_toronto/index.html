Stressed spelled backwards is Desserts, unwind with the special treats of Roselle Toronto. Roselle Desserts is owned by Stephanie Duong and Bruce Lee in the heart of Corktown.
It's Saturday morning and we’ve barely made it out of bed. But the first stop on a lazy day off is Roselle. Corktown's gift from sweet tooth heaven. Both of us are bigger fans of savoury than sweet, but when it comes to these guys, they really have us hooked. The perfect in-between of treats your grandma makes and that Parisian bakery thats saved to your girlfriends’ Pinterest board. Roselle isn’t a huge money buster but it still costs a pretty penny to explore your choices. By this point we’ve mastered our favourites. Two Cappuccinos and a Banana Pie E’Clair. Then begins the cookie mix and match. Saturday we went with the Classic Chocolate Chip Cookie and the Audrey Hepburn Brownie. Take part in the collaborative creative process as we reveal the city through the lens, film your journey, equip it with a soundtrack, and walk away with a one-of-a-kind souvenir from your time here.
